SweetSpot

Welcome to SweetSpot!

We are a group of Harvard engineering students driven to improve the experience of students who... are deaf and hard of hearing. We have designed a tool to select a study/collaboration/work space... tailored to how loud or quiet you would like your space to be. Love working in Lamont, but hate how... loud it is at midnight? Where to work with a pset group without breaking the silence of a room?... Choose a time or location and then volume preference, and find your SweetSpot.
